Herbal Research

project info

This project involves the systematic collection and analysis of data regarding the efficacy of local herbs in treating minor ailments, utilizing Google Sheets for data management. Due to the need for precise and reliable data, the research necessitates extensive investigation into herbal medicines. Specific inclusion criteria for participants are: adults residing in Western countries with no severe pre-existing conditions, such as HIV or diabetes.

The research process involved entering the name of the minor ailment followed by the term, "herbal medicine" into Google Scholar. I verified that the study was conducted on adults and pertained to Western herbal medicine. Upon meeting these criteria, I reviewed the results or conclusion section to ensure the herbal medicine demonstrated a definitive effect on the ailment. If an article was behind a paywall, I accessed the excerpt using the DOI through an alternative website. Finally, I meticulously entered the findings into the herbal research spreadsheet.
