Tax Returns

project info

In this project, I collected a year's worth of physical tax returns and entered the data into a table in Google Sheets. I recorded the name of each organization involved in the transactions, the amount in Rand, and the type of purchase. After entering all the amounts, I meticulously reviewed the data to identify and correct any entry errors. Finally, I created a formula to verify the accuracy of the final amount.

The final outcome was a comprehensive spreadsheet detailing all relevant transactions from the past year. This organized record ensures that, in the event of an audit, auditors would be highly satisfied with the clear and systematic presentation of the expenditure.
